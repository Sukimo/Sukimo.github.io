Color of Magic

Java2D

This is my first project as a programmer.

I started by following a simple Pac-Man tutorial I found online, then expanded it by adding my own gameplay twist — a territory capture mechanic.

As players move through the maze, they leave a trail of color behind, claiming territory along the way. The goal is to control as much of the map as possible while avoiding enemies.
